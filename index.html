Installation Guide (How to Setup)

Follow these execution steps to boot the firmware module via your console's environment loader:

  1. Power off your console and insert the SD card into your PC / card reader.
  2. Navigate to the luma folder on the root of your SD card.
  3. Inside the luma folder, look for a subfolder named payloads (if it doesn't exist, create it).
  4. Copy the downloaded y_firm_darkfox.firm file into that /luma/payloads/ directory.
  5. Safely eject the SD card and insert it back into your console.
  6. Hold down the (Start) button on the console and press the Power button.
  7. The Luma3DS chainloader menu will appear. Select y_firm_darkfox to launch your bare-metal game!
